Pasta'Beare

Wow your friends and family! Learn how to microwave like the pros!

The Pasta'Beare method of pasta prep is sweeping the nation. This recipe outlines how you can bring this tasty and exotic meal to your family's dinner table.

Preparation:

You will need the following:

  • Genuine Pasta'Beare
  • A hunting knife (Find a great selection at knifecountryusa.com)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by boiling the pasta'beare in a spaghetti pot of boiling water overnight. The boiling helps ensure all potential germs and parasites are destroyed.
  2. Then, dry out the pasta'beare with a germ resistant rag.
  3. Next, pop the pasta'beare in the microwave oven for 45 minutes. This helps kill any remaining viruses or parasites remaining.
  4. Let cool.

Serving Instructions:

  1. Then, cut open the pasta'beare with your hunting knife.
  2. Visually inspect the pasta inside for any insects or parasites that may still be inside.
  3. Add alfredo sauce.

Reminder:

Discard the pasta'beare after serving to avoid the risk of spreading disease.
