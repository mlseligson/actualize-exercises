Me Me: My Life In A Pistachio Shell

Background:

Oh, I'm sorry! My name is Matthew! I come from good ole home of the Jayhawks and the wildcats. I'm from Cinco - yep, you guessed it!

They're Quail! I don't know why the eggs are so small. I'm not a scientist after all.
